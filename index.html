The Lenovo Thinkpad X230

A Fan Favourite

With its lightweight, slim design and long battery life, the X230 travel easily, whether to another meeting or another country. Yet with their legendary ThinkPad performance, you sacrifice nothing in return.

Hardware/Design

With its matte black plastic shell, rigid body and demure ThinkPad badges, the x230 offers a refined, functional aesthetic. Moreover, the X230 hosts a pair of USB 3.0 sockets, a VGA port, a Mini DisplayPort, a 54mm ExpressCard slot, and the hardware wireless switch. On the right you'll find a powered USB 2.0 port, a headphone jack, multi-format card reader, the Kensington lock slot, and a Gigabit Ethernet socket.

Display

With its 12.5-inch display, and 300-nit matte IPS panel, the X230 doesn’t disappoint. Its horizontal and vertical viewing angles promises consumers to give the best comfortability.

Performance

With its Intel Ivy Bridge Series, the X230 doesn’t fail to impress and keep up with modern computers today. In addition, upgradability is available for consumers who want to achieve better performance.
